About Show Rater

Show Rater is a simple and intuitive app that lets you track the latest shows you've watched. Add titles, genres, streaming platforms, and personal ratings to build your own collection of reviews.

Features

  • Log shows with descriptions, genres, and streaming platforms.
  • Rate and review each show based on your experience.
  • Organize and revisit past ratings to discover trends in your watch history.

Why Use Show Rater?

Whether you're an avid TV enthusiast or just want to remember what you watched last week, Show Rater helps you keep track of your entertainment journey.
